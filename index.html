Hello, I'm Tristan. I am a web developer, and a musician. I'm very thorough when it comes to problem solving, and fast-paced learning. I am currently looking to grow within a full-time position.

Who?

I've always been considered...well, odd and goofy. When I was younger, I took apart/destroyed anything i could get my hands on. I wanted to know exactly how things worked, and what made them work. This passion and love for understanding has followed me into adulthood, and has manifested within my work. I approach all projects with the same high level of focus, and a desire to learn something new. I love simplicity, coding, working on personal projects as well as working with/for others, tinkering and figuring things out, and playing music.

Work

My most recent venture, Birdshed, is an app that will pull from an index of transcribed solos from well known jazz artists. Transcriptions will come with an in depth theory analysis of the music. This idea stemmed from hearing many young local instrumentalists (including myself) talk about their troubles with knowing what exactly what to practice. I will be using this project to become more familiar with Ajax.

Having never done a project with so few specifications, this was definitely a welcomed challenge. Mr. Moore requested that the site be simple, and able to grow as his business grows. mooremusicrepair.com
